Fruits of the Spirit 🍎
"The fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control."
— Galatians 5:22–23
🍎 1 of 9
❤️ Love
"The fruit of the Spirit is LOVE…"
Love means caring for others and sharing what you have! 💝
When we love like God loves us, we are kind, generous, and always there for each other.
🍋 2 of 9
😊 Joy
"…JOY…"
Joy is a happy feeling deep in your heart that never goes away! 🌟
God gives us joy — not just when things go right, but ALL the time, because He loves us!
🫐 3 of 9
🕊️ Peace
"…PEACE…"
Peace is feeling calm and safe, even on hard days. 🕊️
God's peace is like a warm hug for your heart — it helps you feel still and not afraid.
🍊 4 of 9
⏳ Patience
"…PATIENCE…"
Patience means waiting with a happy heart — not rushing or getting grumpy! 🌱
Just like a tree takes time to grow big and make fruit, good things are worth waiting for!
🍓 5 of 9
🤝 Kindness
"…KINDNESS…"
Kindness is helping others when they need it — even when it's hard! 🩹💗
A small act of kindness — like helping a friend who falls — can make someone's whole day better!
🍏 6 of 9
✨ Goodness
"…GOODNESS…"
Goodness means doing what is right, even when no one is watching! ✅
Helping an elderly neighbour, picking up litter, sharing your lunch — that's goodness in action!
🍇 7 of 9
🙏 Faithfulness
"…FAITHFULNESS…"
Faithfulness means trusting God and keeping your promises! 🙏💜
God is always faithful to us. We can talk to Him every day and trust that He hears us!
🫧 8 of 9
🌸 Gentleness
"…GENTLENESS…"
Gentleness means being soft and careful with people, animals, and all living things! 🐦
Use gentle hands, gentle words, and a gentle voice — it shows how much you care!
🍒 9 of 9
💪 Self-Control
"…and SELF-CONTROL."
Self-control means choosing to do the right thing, even when something tempting is right there! 💪
Waiting until dinner for cookies, sharing the last piece, speaking kindly when upset — that's self-control!
You can grow all 9 fruits! 🌳
